About Me

Welcome to my portfolio! First things first, if you must know anything about me, you should know I am a HUGE fan of pizza, dogs, and of course, graphic design. I have a B.S. from Kansas State University (Go Cats) in Apparel Marketing and I have a minor in Business Administration. Wait, what?? Apparel, fashion? You read that right, I worked in a t-shirt store doing graphic design, where I fell in love with clothing. However, my heart is in content, graphic design, and writing. Any way we can find a way to combine it all?

A little nerd at heart, I grew up teaching myself how to use programs like Paint (LOL), GIMP (props if you know what this is), HTML and CSS coding, and ran a "button/graphics" page on Neopets when I was in middle school. In high school I moved on to better things, like the Adobe Suite and I haven't looked back.

When I'm not working, I like to update my Spotify playlists, find new smoothie or tea places, keep up-to-date with sports, and watch anything that will make me laugh. If you need anything at all, don't hesitate to contact me using the contact link -- I'd love to hear from ya! Enjoy scrolling through what projects I've been up to and feel free to stay awhile.
